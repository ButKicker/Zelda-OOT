# Zelda: Ocarina of Time — 100% Interactive Checklist

A detailed, browser-based interactive checklist for achieving 100% completion in *The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time*. Built as a single self-contained HTML file with no dependencies or installation required.

## Live Demo

**[View the Checklist](https://butkicker.github.io/Zelda-OOT/OoT_100_Checklist.html)**

## Features

- **Comprehensive coverage** — Every item required for 100% completion, organized by location and story progression
- **Categorized tracking** — Separate counters and filters for:
  - Gold Skulltulas (100 total)
  - Heart Pieces & Containers
  - Songs
  - Key Items
- **Live progress bar** — Overall completion percentage updates as you check items off
- **Filter & Search** — Filter by category (Skulltulas, Hearts, Songs, Items, Remaining) or search by name
- **Detailed hints** — Each item includes in-game location hints and tips
- **Context badges** — Items are labeled with helpful flags:
  - `Night` — requires nighttime
  - `Past` — requires Child Link (pre-Master Sword)
  - `Cost` — Rupee cost shown
  - `Warn` — timed or missable item
- **Auto-save** — Progress is saved automatically in your browser's local storage; your checklist persists between sessions
- **Collapsible sections** — Collapse/expand individual locations or all at once
- **Mobile friendly** — Responsive layout works on phones and tablets

## How to Use

### Online (GitHub Pages)
Just click the live link above — no setup needed. Your progress saves automatically in your browser.

### Locally
1. Download `OoT_100_Checklist.html`
2. Open it in any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
3. No internet connection required after download

> **Note:** Progress is stored in your browser's local storage. Clearing browser data or switching browsers will reset your progress.
